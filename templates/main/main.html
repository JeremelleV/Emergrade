The Fashion Waste Crisis

Fast fashion is destroying our planet. We're here to change that.

Fast Fashion's Environmental Impact

The fashion industry is the second largest polluter in the world, just after the oil industry. Every year, millions of tons of clothing are discarded, with a significant portion ending up in landfills or being shipped to developing countries.

Impulse purchases account for a large percentage of fashion waste, as consumers buy items they don't need or that don't fit properly, only to discard them after minimal use.

Our Innovative Solution

Combining cutting-edge technology with sustainable practices

Virtual Try-On Technology

Our advanced VTON system allows you to see how clothes will look on your body before purchasing, reducing returns and ensuring better fit satisfaction.

Brainwave Monitoring

Using the Muse 2 headset, we analyze your brain activity to detect impulse purchasing patterns and provide real-time alerts to encourage mindful shopping.

Sustainable Impact

By reducing impulse purchases and improving fit accuracy, we help decrease clothing waste and promote a more sustainable fashion ecosystem.

Personalized Insights
